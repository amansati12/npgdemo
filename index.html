FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NPG-Unique Computers?
NPG-Unique Computers is the largest one-point Computer Education Institute in Porbandar, established in 2001. We are an Authorised Training Centre for NIELIT, Tally, Microsoft, AutoCAD and more, with 22+ years of teaching experience.
Which courses do you offer?
We offer CCC, CCC+, Tally Essential, Tally Professional, Miracle, Microsoft Office, AutoCAD 2D, AutoCAD 2D+3D, CorelDRAW, Photoshop, Paint, Gujarati Typing and Industrial Training programs.
Are the certificates government-recognised?
Yes. Our NIELIT (CCC/CCC+) certificates are issued by the Government of India. Tally certificates are authorised by Tally Solutions. All certificates are recognised by employers across Gujarat and India.
Is fee installment available?
Yes, we offer an easy fee installment facility so that quality computer education is within everyone's reach, regardless of financial background.
How is the infrastructure at the institute?
We have a world-class 5000 sq. ft. facility spread over two floors, including an air-conditioned computer lab with 30 individual PCs, two classrooms, an audio-visual room with LCD projector, library, canteen, and a reception/counselling area.
